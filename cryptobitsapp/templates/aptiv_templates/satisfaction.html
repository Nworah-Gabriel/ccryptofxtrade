Questions about Satisfaction Guarantee

While the guarantee covers a wide range of fees and commissions, there are services to which it does not apply. These include accounts managed by investment advisors who are not affiliated with APTIV Bank, such as through APTIV Bank Advisor Network® and Managed Account Select®. The guarantee also does not apply to operating expenses charged by mutual funds and ETFs, or interest paid on margin loans. If you have questions on whether the guarantee applies to any particular fee, please call a APTIV Bank investment professional for assistance.

Refund requests must be received within 90 days of the date the fee was charged.

Funds will be credited to the account charged in approximately four weeks of a valid request.
