HOW IT WORKS

What is Hodlvault Vault? Hodlvault Vault is a one-click earning service for your Hodlvault holdings. It is a capital-guaranteed investment product with an easy-to-use interface. It can help users maximize the income potential of their Hodlvault from both centralized and decentralized finance by integrating various Binance products within the Binance ecosystem. Also, it allows Hodlvault HODLers to maximize the yields and receive all the benefits from every opportunity available. With just one click on Hodlvault Vault, your Hodlvault holdings will be flexibly allocated in different products to help you maximize the potential rewards!.



Why should I use Hodlvault Vault?

• Easy to stake.
• Enjoy multiple income earnings from the Binance ecosystem with just one click.
• Flexible redemption whenever you want.

• Daily Hodlvault Vault Income = New tokens rewarded from Launchpool + Hodlvault Flexible Savings + DeFi Staking & more.

Please Note

• You can view your Hodlvault Vault assets and income from [Hodlvault Vault] - [Assets & Withdrawals].
• Income is calculated daily and will be distributed to the user’s Spot account.
• The Hodlvault Vault income will begin to accrue interest at 0:00 AM (UTC) on the second day of staking, and the income will be paid from the third day.

• The staked Hodlvault assets in Hodlvault Vault will be deposited in Flexible Savings and are still eligible for Hodlvault holding benefits from the Daily Hodlvault Balance Calculation, such as airdrop, Launchpad holding position, VIP rights, etc.



OTHER
QUESTIONS

Is there a maximum subscription limit?

There is no maximum limit.


Is Hodlvault Vault a guaranteed investment?

Yes, it is.


How to calculate my income?

Daily Hodlvault Vault Income = New tokens rewarded from Launchpool + Hodlvault Flexible Savings + DeFi Staking & more.

For example, a user stakes 100 Hodlvault in Hodlvault Vault, the user will enjoy the following triple benefits:

• Receive spot income of Hodlvault Flexible Savings every day based on the 100 Hodlvault quota;
• Receive new tokens rewarded from Launchpool based on the 100 Hodlvault quota.
• Receive income from DeFi staking and more, such as from BSC on-chain mining based on the proportion of holdings in the Hodlvault Vault.


When and where can I check my income?

All rewards will be distributed to the user's Spot Wallet on the next day.


How much is the estimated APY of Hodlvault Vault?.

The APY will be adjusted according to the actual benefits and the risk level of Hodlvault Flexible Savings, DeFi Staking, Launchpool, and future potential Earn products.


Can I redeem the staked Hodlvault in advance?.

Yes, the redemption time is flexible.


Is the staked Hodlvault in Hodlvault Vault included in the Hodlvault Balance Calculation Mechanism?.

Yes, the staked Hodlvault in Hodlvault Vault is included in the Hodlvault Balance Calculation Mechanism and is still eligible for Hodlvault holding benefits from the Daily Hodlvault Balance Calculation, such as airdrop, Launchpad holding position, VIP rights, etc.


Security Tips

EMAIL AND PASSWORD

Use a unique email address and password for your account. You can use a secure password manager like LastPass or KeePass to easily keep track of your passwords and create more complex, secure passwords.

Enable 2FA on your Binance account

2FA adds an extra layer of protection to your account in case your password is compromised. Read more here.

Domain name

Always make sure to check the domain address you are visiting: https://www.hodlvault.io

Secure your email account and phone, and use PGP

Enable fingerprint or passcode lock on your phone, use a secure email provider, and use 2FA for your email account. Whenever possible, use PGP

Secure your computer

Don’t install unnecessary software on your computer, or software from untrusted developers. If possible, consider using a dedicated computer or partition for Binance and trading. We recommend installing Linux, Chrome, a password manager plugin, and nothing else.

Secure your internet connection

Always use a wired connection if possible. If you must use WiFi, consider routing your traffic through a VPN.
